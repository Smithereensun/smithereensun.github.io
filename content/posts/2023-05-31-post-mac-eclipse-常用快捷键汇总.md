<ol>
<li>寻找类：shift+command+t</li>
<li>删除当前行：command+d</li>
<li>上移当前行代码：option+↑</li>
<li>下移当前行代码：option+↓</li>
<li>复制当前行至下一行：option+command+↓</li>
<li>复制当前行至上一行：option+command+↑</li>
<li>格式化代码：shift+command+f</li>
<li>抽取本地变量：option+command+L (<span style="color: rgba(255, 0, 0, 1)"><strong>win：control+1</strong></span>)</li>
<li>查看继承关系：command+t</li>
<li>快速找到类：shift+command+t</li>
</ol>
