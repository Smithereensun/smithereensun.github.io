控制台：

```text
vim ~/.bash_profile
```

添加如下配置

```text
export JAVA_13_HOME=/Library/Java/JavaVirtualMachines/jdk-13.0.2.jdk/Contents/Home
export JAVA_8_HOME=/Library/Java/JavaVirtualMachines/jdk1.8.0_211.jdk/Contents/Home
export JAVA_HOME=$JAVA_13_HOME

alias jdk8='export JAVA_HOME=$JAVA_8_HOME'
alias jdk13='export JAVA_HOME=$JAVA_13_HOME'
```

保存后：source ~/.bash_profile

来回切换

1、先输入jdk8或者jdk13

2、java -version

![](/imported/posts/2020-04-21-12748134-0aa86da7-mac-设置多个版本jdk/images/img_001_58f943c5d895.gif)

移除jdk

```text
sudo rm -r -f /Library/Java/JavaVirtualMachines/jdk1.8.0_211.jdk
```
