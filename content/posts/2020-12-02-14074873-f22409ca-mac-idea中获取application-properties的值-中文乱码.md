## 设置idea配置

![](/imported/posts/2020-12-02-14074873-f22409ca-mac-idea中获取application-properties的值-中文乱码/images/img_001_86bfef005959.png)

**将Properties Files (*.properties)下的Default encoding for properties files设置为UTF-8，将Transparent native-to-ascii conversion前的勾选上**

## 新建application.properties

　　将原先的application.properties中的内容备份下，删掉application.properties并新建application.properties文件，将备份的在粘贴进去即可~
