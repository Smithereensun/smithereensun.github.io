# 项目依赖

## Dubbo依赖

```text
        <!--Dubbo依赖-->
        <dependency>
            <groupId>com.alibaba</groupId>
            <artifactId>dubbo</artifactId>
            <version>2.6.7</version>
        </dependency>
        <!--netty-->
        <dependency>
            <groupId>io.netty</groupId>
            <artifactId>netty-all</artifactId>
            <version>4.1.32.Final</version>
        </dependency>
```
