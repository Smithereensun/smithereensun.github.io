# 查询被锁表信息

![](/imported/posts/2020-04-29-12800777-4b22e8da-oracle-锁表查询和解锁方法/images/img_002_52883c6904b8.png)

```text
select sess.sid,
       sess.serial#,
       lo.oracle_username,
       lo.os_user_name,
       ao.object_name,
       lo.locked_mode     from v$locked_object lo,
       dba_objects        ao,
       v$session          sess where ao.object_id = lo.object_id and lo.session_id = sess.sid;
```
